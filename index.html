Who We Are

We're wildlife biologists and wildlife technology experts and our philosophy is to observe, research and showcase unique glimpses of UK and world wildlife in a non-intrusive and respectful way, and give others the skills, expertise and kit to do the same.

We use the most advanced wildlife camera and trail camera tech in conservation projects all over the world, provide wildlife monitoring packages and sell the best kit we use with unparalleled support and service. But we don't want to keep your money… we're a little bit unconventional like that. We're a social enterprise and entirely not for profit -- 100% of our profits goes back to nature.

Our goal is simple; to enable you and our wilder neighbors to live a wild life.
